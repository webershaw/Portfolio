Question Yourself. Be Self Aware

As you might probably know from the intro, my name is Vishal Shaw. I am chasing my dream to become a Web Developer.

Web Development always intrigued during my college days but I was leaning towards Software Development as all my friends. The reason, obviously, because of industry demand and over-exaggerated word of mouth. Unfortunately, I never became a software developer and end up taking a non-IT job due to unforseen circumstances. As the saying goes, "Better Late Than Never", I am back to pursuing the career which I always liked. I know my story is somewhat clich√©, happens to millions of people but believe me it takes a lot of courage to switch your career, at least for me.

Here is an interesting story of my transition. One day, I got a video suggestion from YouTube. The video was titled "Chasing Your Dream" or something similar. I usually do not watch inspirational videos because of a dumb reason that nothing can inspire me. I know, How fool of me? Nonetheless, out of curiosity, I clicked on the video link. To my surprise, the video never tried to inspire me but forced me to ask myself two questions - What am I doing? and What is my future? These two questions brought self-awareness in me. Suddenly, I started questioning myself and my abilities. Speaking of questions, are you bored of my story? I am, just by writing it. So, to summarize my story, I end up getting inspired by the video though it never tried to, which I hate and at the same time grateful, and now I am a Web Developer in making.

Lastly, if it matters, I have a bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering. As a side note, I still ask this question, Why the hell, I am called an engineer. The word, engineer, derived from the word, "engine" but I have nothing to do with engines. Anyway, you can ignore this question. It's just the non-sense inside my head talking.
